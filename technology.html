Manufacturing Technology

Building platforms that transform how products are designed, manufactured, and brought to market

Summary

Elihuu's design-to-manufacture matching software served as the foundation for BriteHub, a transformative platform in supply chain management for product design and manufacturing. These ventures revolutionized how buyers and suppliers connect and collaborate, bringing products to market more efficiently.

Based in Oakland, CA, Elihuu and BriteHub resolved longstanding challenges in the design-to-manufacturing space, enhancing real-time collaboration in purchase order management, minimizing inventory shortages, and optimizing manufacturing processes.

Contributions

Spearheaded development of a web application using agile methodologies, enhancing supply chain management with advanced RFQ and supplier network software.

Secured a substantial portion of a $2.5 million grant from the US Department of Commerce and established a pivotal partnership with Autodesk, integrating BriteHub with their Fusion 360 CAD software.

Executed comprehensive supplier portal solutions for clients in electronics, aerospace, and advanced manufacturing sectors, streamlining procurement and production workflows.
